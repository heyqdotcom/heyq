  # HeyQ

  HeyQ is the PM tool for the vibe coding era. Define your product truth once — your vision, scope, decisions, and boundaries — and every AI tool in your workflow stays aligned. Stop re-explaining. Start shipping what you meant.

  HeyQ captures the product context that usually lives in your head (and gets lost between AI sessions) and serves it to Cursor, Claude, Lovable, v0, and any tool that speaks MCP — so every new session starts informed, not cold.

## Core Capabilities

- **Q — Your AI Product Partner** - Not a chatbot. A collaborator that knows your entire product. Q helps you define product truth, shape strategy, and generate aligned work items — grounded in everything you've already decided.
- **Mission Boards** - Organize work by area (Development, GTM, Business) with *missions* (units of work) and *operations* (grouped missions). Mission boards stay downstream of your product truth, so what you ship matches what you meant.
- **Dedicated Page Types** - Structured pages for the things that actually drive a product: **Decision Log**, **Roadmap**, **Changelog**, **Assets**, and **Links**. Each one captures its domain properly — no more "everything is a doc."
- **MCP Server** - Connect Cursor, Claude Desktop, Claude Code, and any MCP-compatible tool directly to your project. Your product truth flows natively into every AI session — no copy-paste, no re-explaining.
- **Browser Extension** - Trigger Q from any web page. Send the page you're looking at (or a selection) straight into a mission, decision, or Q conversation — great for capturing inspiration, research, and competitive context on the fly.
- **Slack Integration** - Bring Q into the conversations where product actually happens. Capture decisions, spin up missions, and keep your team aligned without leaving Slack.
- **New Project Wizard** - Start in one of 8 different ways — from a blank brief, a landing page, a Product Hunt launch, a roadmap, a growth lab, and more. Whatever shape your product is in, HeyQ meets you there.

## Available Interfaces

HeyQ lives on the web and connects into your AI stack through MCP and integrations:

### Web App
The full HeyQ experience — product truth, mission boards, decision log, roadmap, Q chat, and workspace management.

- Sign up and use HeyQ at **[heyq.com](https://heyq.com)**

### Browser Extension (Chrome)
Trigger Q from any page in your browser. Capture context, spin up missions, and send web content into your HeyQ workspace.

### MCP Server
Expose your HeyQ project to any MCP-compatible AI tool (Cursor, Claude Desktop, Claude Code, and more).

### Slack
Run Q in Slack to keep decisions and missions in sync with where your team talks.

For detailed information on all interfaces, visit [heyq.com/docs](https://heyq.com/docs).
